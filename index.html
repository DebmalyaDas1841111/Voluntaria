OUR FEATURES

Recruitment

Voluntaria includes features to help you promote volunteering oppurtunities, provide application forms, and easily add applicants to the database.

Scheduling and Tracking

For organizing any event, time management is always a major issue. Voluntaria comes to the rescue by helping you to easily add and schedule events based on date,time and location.

Performance Analysis

With an event where a large no. of people are involved, many a times the contributions of the volunteers go unnoticed. So, Voluntaria provides the report acknowledging their contributions.



Registration

With too many important events going on all around the country, voluntaria helps you to register, search and get notifications for events according to your own preference. It allows you to check out the profiles of various organizations.

Task Operation and Organization

Voluntaria helps a volunteer in organising & choosing tasks and helps in maintaining work progress. A record is kept of the work done by the volunteer and ensures that the volunteer's efforts don't go unnoticed.

Profile Builder

With an event where a large no of people are involved, many a times the contributions of the volunteers go unnoticed. But with the performance analysis system in place Voluntaria helps in building up the profile of a volunteer with various credit points and credits.
